My Book Reviews

Atomic Habits – James Clear

A practical guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones through small, consistent changes.

  • Focuses on habit stacking and tiny habits for long term change.
  • Emphasizes systems over goals for sustainable results.
  • Offers tracking and environment design techniques.
Buy on Amazon

Rich Dad Poor Dad – Robert Kiyosaki

Explores financial education through contrasting lessons from the author’s “rich dad” and “poor dad.”

  • Explores mindset differences about money between the wealthy and middle class.
  • Stresses acquiring assets, not liabilities.
  • Encourages entrepreneurial thinking and passive income.
Buy on Amazon

A Guide to Investing – Robert Kiyosaki

Provides strategies for building wealth and making informed investment decisions.

  • Focuses on income-generating assets like real estate and business ownership.
  • Emphasizes understanding investment risk and rewards.
  • Teaches the mindset difference between investors and consumers.
Buy on Amazon

Mindset – Carol S. Dweck

Explores the concept of fixed vs growth mindsets and how they impact success and learning.

  • Growth mindset motivates embracing challenges and learning.
  • Fixed mindset stops personal development and self-belief.
  • Includes strategies to develop a growth-oriented mindset.
