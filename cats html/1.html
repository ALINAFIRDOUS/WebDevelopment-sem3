About

Cats are graceful, independent animals known for their playful charm and quiet companionship. With soft fur, sharp senses, and a love for cozy naps, they’ve become beloved pets across the world. Whether chasing toys or curling up in sunbeams, cats bring warmth and joy to any home.

life stages

kitten
:(0–6 months): Rapid growth, playful, learning social skills.
Junior
(6 months–2 years): Reaches sexual maturity, still energetic.
Adult
(2–6 years): Fully grown, settled personality
mature
(7–10 years): Slight slowdown in activity, needs regular health checks.
senior
(11–14 years): May show signs of aging, needs more care.
